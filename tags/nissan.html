Fiat and Nissan Renault look to partner up for survival

By Ellec on Monday the 27th of May, 2019 at 03:05PM @ellec_uk

Ailing legacy automaker Fiat Chrystler is looking to partner up in a merger worth $37 billion with scandal magnet Nissan/Renault.

The move is widely seen as a survival move as the world moves from Internal Combustion Engines to Battery Electric Vehicles
