Fiat and Nissan Renault look to partner up for survival

By Ellec on Monday the 27th of May, 2019 at 03:05PM @ellec_uk

Ailing legacy automaker Fiat Chrystler is looking to partner up in a merger worth $37 billion with scandal magnet Nissan/Renault.

The move is widely seen as a survival move as the world moves from Internal Combustion Engines to Battery Electric Vehicles

Recently it was announced Fiat were looking to avoid billions in penalties from the EU for it's derisory emission free offerings. To do this they will pay Tesla over $2 billion over the next few years.

This is a survival move for both legacy automakers, strength in numbers and volume.However this move will benefit only Tesla as the volume of cars will be much larger and the new merged company will have to pay Tesla more to offset EU penalties.
