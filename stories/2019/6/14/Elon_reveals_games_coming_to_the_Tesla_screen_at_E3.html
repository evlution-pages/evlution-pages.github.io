Elon reveals games coming to the Tesla screen at E3

By @Ellec on Fri, 14th of June, 2019 at 02:06PM ET

At the 2019 E3 conference Elon Musk sat down with Bethesda's Todd Howard to discuss how gaming inspired him to learn how to code. As part of the discussion it was revealed by Howard they are working with Tesla to bring modern games to the Tesla screen.

Gamification of the Car

Tesla cars already come with some classic games from the Atari days, and also the infamous fun apps such as the fart app. But the new games promise to be more immersive and closer to console style gaming.

The first game shown was the classic Disney style cartoon game Cuphead which can be contollled from a PS4 or XBOX console controller . Another game which will really benefit from the steering wheel input is Beach Buggy Racing 2. Checkout the video below to see them in action.

The new games suggest Tesla is moving towards their cars being entertainment machines rather than just cars, given Full Self driving is just around the corner, Tesla is quickly taking disrupting the transport industry with these bold moves.
