Porsche EV Taycan is canned until 2020

By @Ellec on Wed, 19th of June, 2019 at 02:06PM ET

A Porsche customer is reporting he has been told that their "Tesla killer" will not now be presented until 2020 at the earliest.

The distraught customer posted the information on a forum dedicated to the eternally delayed but highly anticipated vehicle.

It's no surprise the legacy auto companies are having difficulty launching their EV products, Tesla is 4-8 years ahead and anything that has been revealed so far has been a damp squib in terms of spec and looks compared to the 3 models from Tesla.
