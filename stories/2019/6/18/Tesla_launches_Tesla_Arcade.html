Tesla launches Tesla Arcade

By Ellec on Tuesday the 18th of June, 2019 at 07:06PM @ellec_uk

Tesla annonced via Twitter today that the Model 3 is becoming a gaming platform.

Your next charging session is going to be SO 👏 MUCH 👏 FUN 👏 pic.twitter.com/5YzSL36kCC

— Tesla (@Tesla) June 18, 2019
The Tesla twitter account said that the Beach Buggy Racing 2 game by Vector Unit would be rolling out to their cars today.

Your next charging session is going to be SO 👏 MUCH 👏 FUN 👏 pic.twitter.com/5YzSL36kCC

The company is having an "Experience the Tesla Arcade event" in late June in showrooms globally.

Bring your friends and family to a Tesla showroom to experience the new Tesla Arcade until June 30.

Access the full library of games directly from the vehicle’s touchscreen to play gaming classics like Atari Missile Command and Asteroids – or experience our newest addition, Beach Buggy Racing 2, a kart racing game where you can careen, blast and launch your way through 22 tracks. Players can recruit new drivers (see if you can find our favorite) and use the steering wheel controls for the most immersive gaming experience.

If you’re interested in stopping by, kindly RSVP and visit a store near you.

Learn more about the latest performance features during your visit or schedule a test drive for the real thing.
