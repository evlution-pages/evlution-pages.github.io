Volkswagen Battery Woes

By Ellec on Sunday the 28th of April, 2019 at 11:04AM @ellec_uk

Dieselgate company Volkswagen is making changes to it's batter supply plans.

The potetial suppliers of their big move to electric on the heels of Tesla's success in the space, include Samsung. Reports are that Samsung where due to deliver 20GWh annually but that has been slashed to just 5GWh

For comparision Tesla, the largest EV and battery producer can output 35GWh a year from it's Nevada Gigafactory 1, with another Gigactory almost build in China, Volkswagen have a long way to go in their move to EV's

It's clear that legacy auto manufacturers have finally woken up andsmelled the coffee, but are finding the pivot to electric is harder thananticipated, especially after giving Tesla and others a massive head start.
